BASMATI

Basmati rice is a type of rice grown in the Himalayas and Pakistan. It is a long grain rice that is characterized by a light nutty flavor. It is remarkably long for a rice and becomes longer still when cooked. It can be either brown or white with the brown variety having more flavor.
