RAW

Fresh paddy is processed and milled to get white raw rice. This rice has good fragrance, Whiteness and looking properties. It has low elongation and more stickiness as compared to Aged and steam rice.

AGED RAW

To get best cooking results like good elongation, less stickiness and more water absorption Ratio the raw rice is aged for 18 months.

STEAM

Fresh or seasoned paddy is processed at contolled steam pressure, temperature and time Depending upon the paddy variety, input paddy physical properties and desired final rice Quality. This rice has good elongation and less stickiness as compared to raw rice.

AGED STEAM

To get better cooking results like good elongation, less stickiness and more water Absorption ratio the steam rice is aged for 10 months.

BROWN

When only the outermost layer of a grain of rice (the husk) is removed, brown rice is produced. To produce white rice, the next layers underneath the husk (the bran layer and the germ) are Removed, leaving mostly the starchy endosperm it is a whole grain with a good source of Magnesium, phosphorus, selenium, thiamine, niacin, vitamin b6, an excellent source of Manganese and high in fiber.
