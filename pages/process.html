Process

Milling rice is a state of art and it needs combination of experience, technology and quality of raw material; which plays major role in determining the final product quality. We at new haryana overseas have focussed and specialized only on steam, raw, aged steam and aged raw basmati rice. Steam and raw grains are very delicate grains as compared to sella or parboil in consideration of milling, it needs specialization in every stage of procurement, handeling, cleaning, pre drying, storage, processing, drying, dehusking and milling of basmati paddy. The process starts from procurement of raw material. For manufacturing of steam and raw rice very healthy paddy is required. We are situated in foothils of himalya where paddy of pure quality is cultivated in the fertile land of punjab & haryana watered with snow fed water from himalayas. Geographical position of our rice mills supports us in procurring the best raw material for process. Very strict monitoring of raw material is done physically and scienctifically by our expert staff.
